I'm A Pilot

I quite enjoy LOVE flying airplanes. I currently hold my Private Pilot License and Glider Pilot Instructor Rating.

During the summer months, I work at the Central Region Gliding School as a flight instructor, teaching both in air lessons and ground school. On weekends during the year, I work at my local gliding center, providing familiarization flights to junior cadets, many of whom have never experienced flight before!

I have also received the accolades of Top Overall Glider Pilot in 2009 while at CRGS and Top Overall Power Pilot at Durham Flying Centre in 2010, also through the Royal Canadian Air Cadet program.

I am honoured to have been selected as part of British Airways' Future Pilot Programme which will be commencing in June 2014.
